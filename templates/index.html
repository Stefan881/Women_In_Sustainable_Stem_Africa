About us

About us

Company History

Welcome to Women in Sustainable STEM Africa, (WiSSA), a community aimed at empowering women to shape a sustainable future through STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields across Africa. We are passionate about promoting gender equality, sustainability, and innovation in STEM fields, and we believe that by harnessing the talents and potential of women, we can create a brighter and more sustainable future for Africa and beyond.

At WiSSA, our mission is to provide a supportive and inclusive community where women in STEM can connect, learn, and thrive. We are committed to breaking down barriers and challenging stereotypes that hinder women's progress in these fields.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

To support, advocate for, and celebrate women in sustainable STEM fields. We aim to promote diversity and inclusivity in STEM, and provide resources and opportunities for women to thrive in these fields. Through mentorship, education, and community-building, we strive to create a network of empowered women who are leading the charge towards a more sustainable future.

To create a world where women are equally represented and empowered in sustainable STEM fields, driving innovation and progress towards a more sustainable future.
